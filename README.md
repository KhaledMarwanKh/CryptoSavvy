# 🚀 CryptoSavvy
Smart Crypto Market Insights Powered by AI

CryptoSavvy is an AI-driven platform that analyzes real-time cryptocurrency market data to predict whether prices may rise or fall. It leverages machine learning, technical indicators, and historical trends to provide actionable insights for smarter trading decisions.

---

## 🔍 What is CryptoSavvy?
CryptoSavvy is an advanced crypto-analysis platform that leverages cutting-edge AI and machine-learning models to help traders make smarter and more confident decisions.

By continuously analyzing:
- Real-time market data  
- Price movements  
- Trading volume  
- Technical indicators  

CryptoSavvy predicts whether a cryptocurrency is likely to rise or fall — empowering users with insights before market shifts occur.

---

## ⭐️ Key Features
✅ AI-powered price prediction  
✅ Real-time market monitoring  
✅ Technical indicator analysis (RSI, MACD, SMA, EMA, etc.)  
✅ Risk assessment and trend forecasts  
✅ Supports BTC, ETH, and major altcoins  
✅ Clear insights for beginners + professionals  

---

## 🎯 Why CryptoSavvy?
CryptoSavvy transforms complex blockchain & market data into easy-to-understand insights that help traders stay ahead of volatility and make data-driven decisions with confidence.

The platform is built with secure cloud infrastructure, optimized for speed, and designed with a clean, intuitive user interface.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- Backend: Python, Node.js / Express
- ML Models: Deep Learning + Technical Indicators
- Data: Real-time + Historical crypto market feeds
